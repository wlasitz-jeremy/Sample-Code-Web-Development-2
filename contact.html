Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official Disney site?

Absolutely not. This is a fan page created as a student web development project. All characters and related materials are the property of The Walt Disney Company.

Can I contribute content?

We love the enthusiasm! Send us a message using the form above and let us know what you'd like to add. We review all suggestions before posting anything.

How long will it take to get a reply?

We're students, so please be patient. We aim to respond within a week, though it may take longer during exam season.

Why The Aristocats specifically?

Honestly? It came up in a random generator. But the more we worked on it, the more we appreciated this underrated gem of the Disney canon. Everybody wants to be a cat.
